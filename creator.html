About Me

Hi there, my name is Viet. I was borned in Vietnam and am currently an international student in Australia. I in my final year as an undergraduate student at The University of Melbourne. Studying the major Computing and Software Systems under the Bachelor of Science. The Koi Corner design was a project for the Visual Communication Design subject at Kew High School. My project and design was selected to be a part of the 31 students that was awarded of Top Design 2021 and put to display at The Melbourne Museum throughout 2021.

Throughout my course at the university, I was learning to become and software engineer. However, I was also interested in web development and started to self-studied HTML5, CSS and JavaScript in 2024. With this new knowledge, I decided to showcases my Koi Corner design and work through a website. This project is not only about the physical appearance and final presentation of my design.
