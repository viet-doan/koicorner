About Me

Born in Vietnam, Viet is currently an international student in Australia, pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computing and Software Systems at The University of Melbourne. Viet's academic pursuits reflect his passion for technology and innovation, and he is committed to lifelong learning and expanding his knowledge and skills. His career goal is to work in a dynamic and innovative company where he can contribute to the development of cutting-edge technology solutions.

Viet's Koi Corner design was selected as one of the 31 students to be awarded with Top Design 2021 and displayed at The Melbourne Museum. Throughout his course at the university, Viet was learning to become a software engineer. However, he was also interested in web development and started to self-study HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript in 2024. The website demonstrated his ability to integrate his design skills with web development, while displaying the art work.
