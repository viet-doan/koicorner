Drink Container Design

Purpose

The design and drawings of the Koi Corner's reusable drink container should depict how the container functions and looks, to serve as a guide for the manufacturer to create the product. The drink container's purpose is to function as a reusable container that can maintain the drink's temperature to maintain the flavours and freshness of the drinks up to 12 hours. The secondary purpose of this product is to advertise summertime specials whilst promoting Koi Corner as a sustainable brand to keep up waste-reduction opportunity.

Constraints

There were some other constraints and considerations that were made during the process of design the drink container for Koi Corner. The product should also be BPA free and made up with food-grade stainless steel and withstand daily uses for the lifespan of at least 5 years. The container cannot have any sharp edges to ensure that the user can drink from the container safely without injuring themselves. The width of the bottle should be narrow enough so that the user can grip on the container tightly without it slipping easily.
