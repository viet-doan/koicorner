About Us

Koi Corner is a renowned bubble milk tea chain that was born and brewed in Japan in 2003. Ever since 2010, their brand was adjusted to target international customers, and was esstablished as a well-recognised worldwide brand with stores located over 25 countries with luxurious and delightful sweet beverages. The figure of a Koi fish is closely tied with Japan's national identity and is the symbol for Koi Corner, instituting its origin and brand identity.

The luxurious aesthetic of the Japanese bubble-milk tea franchise Koi Corner influenced the design of the logo and re-usable drink cjontainer. The elegant symbol of the Koi fish and vertically aligned type, highlights the brand's origin and along with the sophisticated form of the bottle,creates an authentic visual language that promotes the store.
